About us

Launched in the Spring 2014, Marathons and Half Marathons expects to become the go-to resource for runners looking to go 13.1 & beyond. Our unparalleled race calendar will be backed up by magazine-style reviews curated by our team and submitted by workers like you. We'll also be actively encouraging race directors to tell us about their races, which we think will be a really neat way to connect runners and race directors.
